About the Project

This is a Django (GA) General Assembly Immersive Project

PetDesk had its inspiration drawn from the idea of a software solution to check pets into an animal clinic.

Our team of three members had one week to design and implement a Full Stack Django Application using PostgreSQL with (1:M) and (M:M) relationships. Additionally, the application had to support full CRUD data operations while authenticating users with the appropriate authorization.

Please take the time and explore our application.
